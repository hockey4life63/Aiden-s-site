Services

Commercial

  • Professional Locksmithing: Own a restaurant, office building, apartment complex, or other commercial service? We offer world-class locksmithing and round-the-clock emergency services so you never have to sweat another lockout or missing key.

  • ADA Compliance: We can install ADA upgrades, like compliant door openers and closers

  • Security Doors: We also specialize in steel door service and installation

  • Master Keying: Don't get stuck with a ring full of keys! Use one key to open many doors, or personalize your options. We can even create an access specific system that will give you control over which keys can open which locks.

  • Key Control Systems: With patent restricted keyblanks you never have to worry about un-authorized key copies again!

Safes

  • Opening: Our highly trained professional locksmiths can open any safe! We can also repair damaged or malfunctioning safes AND provide annual maintenance to keep your safe as secure as possible.

  • Sales and installation: We also specialize in the sale and installation of professional-grade safes. Proper installation is key to protecting the safe from theft; it's quite difficult to steal a safe that is bolted to the floor.

  • GSA Certified Inspectors: NH Lock and Security is certified by the General Services Administration which allows us to maintain government contracts. This means that our technicians undergo rigorous security checks to ensure that you are being provided the most secure and trustworthy service.

Access Control Systems

  • Modernize your building security and get piece of mind with high-tech access control systems. We specialize in the service and installation of ACS, like key card control, pin code entry, even biometrics!

  • More importantly, we can install a closed-circuit television system (CCTV) which will provide privately monitored round-the-clock surveillance. Our technicians will strategically place the cameras in areas at most risk for entry and walk you through how to monitor the system. Our professional-grade equipment provides high-resolution video and audio, as well as zoom features. Upgraded CCTV even allows you to interact with people in range.
